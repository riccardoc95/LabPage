    TeraLab to Host a Special Session on HPC and Genomics @ CIBB 2025

    2025-09-09

    As part of the upcoming CIBB 2025 – Computational Intelligence Methods for Bioinformatics and Biostatistics conference, to be held in Milan from September 10–12, 2025, TeraLab will organize a Special Session entitled:

    High-Performance Computing for AI-driven Genomics

    The session will focus on the intersection of high-performance computing, deep learning, and large-scale genomic data analysis. Topics include:

    • · modeling and analysis of large genomic datasets
    • · optimization of computational pipelines using HPC and GPU-based infrastructures
    • · application of deep learning models to complex biological problems

    The session aims to foster discussion and collaboration among researchers working on advanced computational methods for genomics, with particular attention to scalability, efficiency, and innovation.
